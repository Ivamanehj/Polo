Billiards
Grip the cue firmly and focus on the target


Ensure a steady and strong stance, focus on your target, and then direct your attention to the cue ball using your cue.
Think about the shot you want to make with the cue ball in order to hit the object ball. To achieve the highest level of accuracy,
it is crucial to strike the cue ball precisely in its center, rather than simply making contact with it. Aiming for the center greatly
enhances your likelihood of striking the object ball. Feel free to relax and ensure that your cue stick and cue ball are properly aligned,
allowing you to clearly see the object ball in your intended path.
