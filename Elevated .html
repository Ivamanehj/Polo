Elevated Bridge

When the cue ball is obstructed by another ball, this technique can come in handy. To successfully execute this shot, just follow these steps:
● Step 1: Position your index finger on the table at a nearly perpendicular angle.
● Step 2: Create a stable base by positioning your pinkie finger on the ground and folding your middle and ring fingers beneath it.
● Step 3: Create a v-shaped channel in the air by raising your thumb upwards, between your thumb and index finger.
● Step 4: Take your shot by firmly gripping the cue with your thumb and index finger.
