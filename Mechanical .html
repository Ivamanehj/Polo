Mechanical bridge

A mechanical bridge can be utilized to help with the shot in situations where the pool ball target is beyond player reach. It is typically the case to play on a larger and wider pool table.
● Step 1: Position the bridge on the table just behind your cue ball, ensuring a distance of six to eight inches for a seamless shot.
● Step 2: With your other hand, firmly grasp the bridge. For those who are right-handed, it is recommended to position the bridge cue to the left of the shooting cue.
● Step 3: Position your stick in the groove that offers the optimal shot.
● Step 4: Hold the cue's butt cap with your index, thumb, and middle finger. ● Step 5: Position your head towards the desired target and proceed to take the shot.
